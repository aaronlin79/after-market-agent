# Product Specification

## Product

AI-powered morning digest for after-market financial news and SEC filings.

---

## Target User

Retail or professional market participants who want a concise and reliable pre-market briefing.

---

## Core Problem

Important overnight developments are fragmented across:

* news articles
* SEC filings
* duplicate reporting

Users need a single, ranked, digestible summary.

---

## V1 Features

* Watchlist management
* News ingestion
* SEC filings ingestion
* Data normalization
* Story clustering
* Importance ranking
* Grounded summaries
* Morning digest delivery (email)
* Digest archive

---

## Non-Goals

* Stock prediction
* Investment advice
* Trading signals
* Real-time alerts
* Social media scraping

---

## Digest Structure

### Must Know

Top high-signal developments

### Watch at Open

Relevant but less confirmed or lower priority

### SEC Filings

Important filings to review

### Likely Noise

Filtered low-signal or duplicate items

---

## Success Criteria

* Digest is concise and readable
* High-signal events are correctly prioritized
* Summaries are accurate and grounded
* Duplicate stories are minimized
* System runs reliably on schedule
